A little about us

Hi! Im Radhika and Im the founder of The Destination Bride Tribe and also international wedding planning company with my hubby called Radhika & You

I literally live and breathe all things weddings. My love for weddings started from a young age as I attended lots of family weddings having big Indian family myself.

Another passion of mine is travel, exploring new countries, trying new food and meeting new people. Any opportunity to travel and I will be already checking flight options, hotels to stay in and creating an itinerary before even booking (yes, Im one of those!).

With my love for weddings and travel combined together it is the main reason we offer both wedding planning in the UK, Europe and Worldwide.

I often speak to couples and read questions from couples about not knowing where to start, a bit overwhelmed with the wedding planning process or just not having enough time with their busy lifestyles.

So, I wanted to create an exclusive free community for brides across the globe planning their destination wedding whether its in the UK, Europe, Africa, America, Middle East and the list goes on!

Come join the Destination Bride Tribe and be part of our community!
